Pizza Recipe

Description

Here is everything you need to know about making a homemade pizza that is very delicious!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cornmeal
  • Cheese
  • Various Pizza Toppings of your choosing

Steps

  1. Make the dough by mixing dough ingredients togethers by hand or stand mixer
  2. Knead by hand or with your mixer
  3. Place dough into greased mxing bowl and set aside to rise for 90 minutes
  4. Punch down risen dough to release air bubbles and roll dough out to desired shape
  5. Top it with your favorite toppings
  6. Bake pizza at high temperature for about 15 minutes
