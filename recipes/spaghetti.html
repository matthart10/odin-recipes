Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a very interesting food. I really like to eat spaghetti when I want a big meal. Here is what you will need to do in order to make spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion, garlic, and spices
  • Water
  • Tomatoes
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large pot
  2. Add meat and cook for about 8 minutes
  3. Add the onions and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Stir garlic, tomato paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes and cook for 1 minute
  5. Pour in water
  6. Stir in tomatoes, salt and black pepper
  7. Cook sauce uncovered for 25 minutes
  8. Boil water 15 minutes before sauce is done
  9. Throw in the pasta and cook according to instructions in packaging
  10. Remove sauce from heat and stir, toss the cooked pasta and leave for one minute
  11. Toss again and serve with cheese on top if desired
